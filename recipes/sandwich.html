PB&J Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jam (flavor of choice)

Directions

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread jam on another slice of bread.
  3. Put slices together and serve fresh.
